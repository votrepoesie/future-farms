Locally Planted, Harvested, and Delivered with Love

Day 1

We grow the seeds by germinating them in peat moss for 3-4 days.

Day 4

We move the seeds into hydroponics, where they grow for the next 7-8 days.

Day 5-9

We check on them everyday to make sure they are healthy!

Day 10

Once the microgreens are ready to be harvested, we cut and package them with care.

Day 11

We deliver straight to your door within 72 hours of harvest!
